━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🧠 INTELLIGENT H2 CREATION PROCESS:

Step 1: READ the full article content carefully
Step 2: UNDERSTAND what the main story is about  
Step 3: IDENTIFY specific details (player names, scores, events, statements)
Step 4: CREATE H2 headings based on those specific details
Step 5: Each H2 should reflect a DIFFERENT aspect of the story

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━


📊 COMPARISON:

❌ OLD APPROACH (Template-Based):
"I'll use these fixed headings for every article"
Result: Generic, boring, same structure everywhere

✅ NEW APPROACH (Intelligent):
"Let me read this article first, understand it, then create relevant headings"
Result: Content-specific, engaging, unique structure

🎯 KEY PRINCIPLES:

1. READ FIRST, CREATE LATER
   Don't generate headings before understanding content

2. SPECIFIC > GENERIC
   "पंत की वापसी" >> "खिलाड़ी प्रदर्शन"

3. CONTENT-AWARE
   H2 should show you understood what article is about

4. UNIQUE FOR EACH ARTICLE
   Different stories = Different H2 structures

5. ENGAGING & CLICKABLE
   Use questions, specific names, intriguing phrases
